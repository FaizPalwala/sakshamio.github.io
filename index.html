Case Study Competition

    Participants will be given cases of real-world scenarios in the corporate space and asked to develop a solution. Students can team up to solve the problems in the given business case by putting themselves in the shoes of a CEO and developing a winning business strategy.

Stock Market Simulation

    The stock market simulator attempts to reproduce features of a live stock market so that a player may practice trading stocks without financial risk. The event requires the participants to maximize returns, by analysing and investing in the given stock market simulator. Participants will be given an equal amount of virtual money to start off the simulated stock market.
