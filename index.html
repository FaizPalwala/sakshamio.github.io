Bulls and Bears : The Finance Club, VIT

Bulls and Bears : The Finance Club, VIT, promotes finance related activities and careers throughout the VIT community. The main objective of this club is to spread awareness in the domain of finance, in particular investment banking, stocks and trading, private wealth management and many more. The club provides a constructive platform for helping the finance aspirants apply theoretical concepts they have learnt in real life

IE(I) : Institute of Engineers(India)

IE(I) : Institute of Engineers(India), renders its services to its members through various technical events and activities by conducting seminars, conferences , workshops etc. Its main aim is to help the members from all the disciplines of engineering to upgrade in their respective fields and also other technical aspects. It also aims to serve a platform for all the budding engineers with different areas of interest. This chapter is in the service of the future engineers and researchers to help explore more areas of technology and science.
